ElevenMusic brings listening and creating into one place. Discover independent and emerging artists curated around your taste, then turn that inspiration into original tracks with AI-supported creation tools powered by ElevenLabs' music model.

Start with a lyric, melody, or mood and generate a full composition you can shape — refine structure, adjust the sound, or remix a track you love by shifting genre, tempo, and arrangement. ElevenMusic is indie-first by design, built to help new artists reach the right listeners and make music creation feel simple, intuitive, and fun.
